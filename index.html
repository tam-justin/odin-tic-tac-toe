Tic Tac Toe!
Play a fun game of tic tac toe!
Enter player's names on the left side. If no name is given, players will be named Player 1 & 2. To start the game, press the start game button. The first player's turn will be chosen at random. When it is your turn, your marker (X or O) will become green. Make your move by clicking on a square. If at any point, you wish to restart the game, click restart game.
